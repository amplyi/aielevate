4. EDMP VALUE CHAIN (use in all content)
================================================================================

  Signal → Structure → Decision → Execution Trace → Institutional Memory

Financial pillars:
  - Risk reduction (audit, litigation, regulatory, AI governance)
  - Reduced rework (same decisions debated repeatedly)
  - Executive continuity (memory survives people leaving)
  - AI governance (reasoning lineage for AI-assisted decisions)

Category positioning:
  CRM = Customer Memory
  ERP = Operational Memory
  EDMP = Decision Memory
  (Layer above dashboards, workflows, and AI platforms — not replacing them)

  1. Executive Capital Decisions (finance)
  2. Commercial Decision Flow (sales)
  3. AI Decision Oversight (governance)
  4. Talent & Hiring Decisions (recruitment)
